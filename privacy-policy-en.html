Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

InterviewCoach AI ("we", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Voice recordings: When you practice an interview, we temporarily record your voice to transcribe and analyze it. We do not store audio recordings — only the text transcription.

Account data: If you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email (optional) to identify your account.

Usage data: We save your practice answers, scores, and progress so we can show your improvement over time.

Payment information: Subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not store credit card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze your answers with artificial intelligence (OpenAI)
  • To show your historical progress
  • To manage your Pro subscription
  • To improve the app experience

3. Artificial Intelligence (OpenAI)

Your voice transcriptions are sent to OpenAI for feedback analysis. OpenAI processes this data under its own privacy policy, available at openai.com/privacy. We do not share your name or personal information with OpenAI — only the text of your answers.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • OpenAI: for answer analysis (text only, without identifying data)
  • RevenueCat: for subscription management
  • Apple: for authentication and payments

5. Data Storage

Your practice data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not have access to your practice data from our servers.

6. Data Retention

You can delete your data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Voice recordings are not stored — they are processed in real time and discarded.

7. Children

InterviewCoach AI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of significant changes to this policy through an app update.

9. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@interviewcoachai.app
